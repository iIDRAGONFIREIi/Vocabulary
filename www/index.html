Vocabulary

Beginning:

Hello, today we are invited to discuss about the following topic:; Hello, the big issue we are talking about today is ...

Introducing myself:

name + age + where you live + hobbies (wenn als Aufhänger für Diskussion)

Agreeing:

I agree; I coudn´t agree more; That´s exactly my point of view, too; I share your point of view; That´s how I see it, too; You name it; Say it again; That´s a good point; I am of the same opinion

Disagreeing:

I disagree; No; I think you are wrong; That´s not how I see it; We differ on that topic; That´s where your opinions differ; You can´t be serious; And how are you going to solve that problem?; That´s utter rubbish!; That´s not practicable

In two minds:

I´m in two minds about that; I still cannot decide which side I am on; Sure; There are good and bad aspects in this matter; Well, I am undecided on that matter

Questioning for details:

I didn´t get it; Could you give me more details?; Let's come back to ...; Could you explain that to me once again?; Can I get some more details on that?

Summing up:

All in all, ...; We can say ...; To put it into a nutshell ...; To cut a long story short ...; Let´s sum that up; Let us summarize all we have found; Can we have a resume on that?

Ending:

What about forming that compromise?; Isn´t ... a compromise we all can agree on?; I think that ... is a solution to the problem we all can live with; To come to an end, wouldn´t ... be a good outcome?
